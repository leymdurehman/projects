Essential George Information

Background

"...For I am Costanza, Lord of the Idiots"

George Louis Costanza was a widely beloved character on the iconic American television show 'Seinfeld'.
From 1989 to 1998, actor Jason Alexander gifted viewers with one of the most expertly crafted characters in television history.
